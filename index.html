General Training or Academic Version?

Not sure of which version to take? It all depends on what you need the exam for. If the exam is a formal requirement, for example, to issue a visa, you must always find out which version you need to take. Also, it important that you know which score band you need to obtain, to make sure you are ready to work on the exam.

General Training

This version is a usual requirement for those willing to live or work in an Engish Speaking countries. The listening and speaking parts are the same for both versions. However, the reading part is different from the academic version because there are holiday or work-related texts,. The first writing, which is a formal or informal letter, is different, too.

Academic

In contrast, the academic version certifies you language competence in order to study in academic contexts, such as language schools or universities. For this reason, the reading comprehension part consist of longer and more specialized texts, while the writing task 1 is a report based on some graphs or tables.
